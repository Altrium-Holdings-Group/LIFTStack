# Discovery Routing

## Hard rule

LIFTStack discovery must never cross-route into another brand wizard by default.

Approved LIFTStack route:

```text
https://www.lift-technology.net/discovery-wizard/
```

## Why this exists

LIFTStack prospects are technical implementation, automation, and tooling prospects. Cross-routing corrupts intake data and weakens delivery accountability.

## LIFTStack intents

LIFTStack accepts these technical intents:

- ai-automation
- workflow-engineering
- app-build
- systems-integration
- internal-tooling
- platform-migration
- technical-rescue
- other

## Post-intake handoffs

A lead can be handed off after intake only when the receipt captures the original LIFTStack context and the handoff is explicit.

Examples:

- Altrium for HoldCo, portfolio, investment, and governance matters
- Schiele & Associates for advisory execution and leadership consulting
- Circle of Growth for protected IP, cohorts, licensing, and certification
- BearSchiele for founder/media/speaking lanes
- Genesis AI Platform for AI product/platform lanes

The primary CTA still remains LIFTStack unless a governed handoff is created.
